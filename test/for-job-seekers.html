How to succeed at interviews?

The key to success in a job interview can be defined through different aspects. The basics include knowing the recruiting company's profile and culture, its turnover, and its strategy for the short and long term. It also involves an in-depth reflection on the job description and one's own motivation for the role.

The STAR technique is a proven method for effectively answering competency-based interview questions. It comprises four steps: Situation, Task, Action, and Results.
